a man-cub named Mowgli. Mowgli is hunted by an evil tiger named Shere Khan.
Mowgli tries to live a peaceful life with other humans, but is too wild for
them and too human for the wolves. Eventually Mowgli finds a home in the jungle
with a pack of his own.
